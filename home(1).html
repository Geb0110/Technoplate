Prologue

In the world we live in now, most of the things are being taken over by AI, and this has skyrocketed to such an extent that AI can even clean houses. So here's our unconventional, yet intriguing and neoteric web application, which uses AI to automate the substitution of mainly teachers in school, or any other professionals and preceptors in a registered and verified organisation.

The Catalyzer

This web app speeds up the process of substitution in numerous ways. In a conventional scenario, for substitutions, teachers have to be called up, sometimes twice! This process can get tedious, and thus to avoid that, our web is made to substitute teachers with the click of a button. It's ability to recognize free and engaged teachers at a specific moment, takes it to a whole different level.

Artificial Intelligence at the right place, at the right time -

For AutoLocum, there's no need of our intervention to call a teacher, it takes charge with it's cutting edge abilites to detect the status of a teacher, even when the teacher is inactive/offline
